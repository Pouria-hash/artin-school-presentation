Online Theory Classes At The Convenience Of Your Home

Our driver instruction course gives participants the opportunity to interact on multiple levels. Practice activities that have been integrated into the course include many scenario-based questions and activities that help to reinforce the material while improving retention of the information.

Learning through our online course gives students a full year of access.

Highly interactive slides, activities, and videos are central to the learning.

Students can learn at their own pace, working from the comfort of their own homes, schools, work, or anywhere, at any time of the day or night.

Artin Driving School provides driver training in Halifax, Dartmouth, Bedford, Sackville, and surrounding areas.

Artin Driving School offers 35-hour (25 hours online theory class and 10 hours in-car practice) Access Nova Scotia approved driver education course. Our courses are available all year round.

Artin Driving School also offers individual in-car practice and road test preparation in our dual-brake-equipped cars with well-trained instructors.

​Artin Driving School instructors are approved and licensed by Access Nova Scotia and are highly trained.
